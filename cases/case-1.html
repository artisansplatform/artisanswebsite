Smart Product Search – Find What You Want - Even When You Don’t Know What It’s Called

AI-powered Smart Product Search understands intent, not just keywords. It goes beyond exact matches to show what the customer really wants - even if they use different words, spell things incorrectly, or mix styles.

Schedule a Demo

The Challenge: Different Words, Same Product

When shoppers search online, they don’t all use the same words. One person might type “blue sneakers” while another says “navy running shoes.”

A basic site search struggles with this. The result? Irrelevant products, frustrated shoppers, and missed sales.

From Guesswork to Perfect Matches - With Smart Related Suggestions

Even when exact matches aren’t available, AI displays the closest related products - ensuring customers always find something that aligns with their intent.

  • Party Look Search
  • Work Bag Suggestions
  • Kidswear Made Easy

Party Look Search

A shopper types “party heels.” The search first shows exact matches like stilettos and block heels. If those aren’t enough, it also suggests glitter heels and sparkly evening shoes to cover related styles.

Work Bag Suggestions

A shopper types “work laptop bag.” Exact matches like slim laptop bags appear first. Alongside, AI suggests leather office bags and messenger bags that serve the same professional need.

Kidswear Made Easy

A shopper types “kids winter jackets.” Exact results like puffer jackets and hooded parkas are displayed first, followed by related options such as fleece hoodies and cozy sweaters to expand the choice.

The Tech That Understands Your Customers

AI-powered search uses natural language understanding and contextual relevance to decode what shoppers mean, not just what they type.

It considers:

  • Synonyms and variations (blue = navy, sneakers = running shoes)
  • Product attributes (style, material, size, color)
  • Search patterns (popular queries, user behavior)

This makes product discovery seamless - even when customers are vague or creative with their words.
