Why TabsyPOS Makes the Perfect Backup POS

Instant Access — No Setup Needed:

When your primary POS goes down, there’s no time for installations or configuration.
With TabsyPOS, you can simply open your browser and start billing immediately — no sign-in, no dependencies.

Fast. Simple. Works on Any Device:

TabsyPOS is designed for speed and flexibility. A sale can be completed in as little as 10 seconds, helping you serve customers quickly even when your main POS is down.

It runs directly in your browser — on any device, including laptops, tablets, or mobile phones. No installation, no setup. Just open and start billing instantly.

Offline-Ready

Even if your internet connection drops, TabsyPOS continues to work smoothly right from your browser. Your transactions are saved locally, ensuring your sales never stop.

Privacy Guaranteed

Your data never leaves your device. Unlike cloud-based POS systems, everything is stored securely in your own browser, maintaining full control over your information.

Export When You’re Ready

Once your main POS system is back online, you can easily export all your sales and customers from TabsyPOS. Import them back into your main POS and continue business as usual — with nothing lost.
