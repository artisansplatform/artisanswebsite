Take your omnichannel operations to next level by connecting your online and offline in one system using our unified commerce solution.

Why Unified Commerce?

Customers appreciate the flexibility of purchasing across multiple channels. However, delivering omnichannel services alone is not enough. You could be missing out on valuable cross-channel insights if your backend systems are not connected. By connecting your systems, you can become a truly unified commerce business.

Achieving Central Truth & Control means having a unified perspective of customer information, order details, product specifications, inventory levels, and pricing data across all sales channels on a single platform.

With this capability, businesses can easily synchronize and manage their inventory across multiple locations, such as warehouses, distribution centers, and physical stores, and efficiently allocate stock for different channels, such as eCommerce websites and online marketplaces, using a single interface.

Our Unified Commerce Platform is built with an API-first approach and on a microservices model to ensure flexibility, scalability, and ease of integration with existing systems. Adopting an API-first approach will enable to connect and share data between commerce channels thereby creating a truly seamless and integrated shopping experience.
